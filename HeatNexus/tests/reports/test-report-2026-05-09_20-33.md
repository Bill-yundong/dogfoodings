**热平衡公式**:
- 供热量: Q_supply = Σ (flow_i * ρ * cp * (T_source - T_return))
- 热损失: Q_loss = Σ heatLoss_i
- 终端热量: Q_end = Σ (flow_i * ρ * cp * (T_outlet - T_return))
- 效率: η = Q_end / Q_supply * 100%
